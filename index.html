About Gurutwa

Started in 2012, Gurutwa is the technical fest organized by the students of SNM institute of Management and Technology, Maliankara with a motto of providing a platform to technical enthusiasts of our country to explore, innovate and showcase their technical and engineering prowess. With bigger scope and advanced ideas, Gurutwa 2020 looks forward to exploring ever widening horizons and surmounting greater heights with a myriad of events spread across a plethora of domains. Gurutwa 2020 promises to be a platform which shall integrate technology with challenging environments. A festival full of promise and celebrations, Gurutwa 2020 shall be a journey more exciting and enthralling than any other to bring out the true passion of an engineer.
